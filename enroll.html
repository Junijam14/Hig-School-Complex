Enroll Your Child

We warmly welcome parents and guardians who wish to enroll their children in our school. Registration is done in person at the school office.

Admission information

Our School provides quality and affordable education for children in our village and surrounding communities.
We focus on discipline, academic excellence and strong moral values.

Requirements for Admission

  • Birth Certificate(Original and Copy)
  • Previous School Report Card ( if transferring)
  • Two Passport-sized Photographs
  • Transfer letter(if applicable)

Admission Period

Admissions are open at the beginning of every academic term.
Parents are encouraged to visit early to secure placement.

School Office Hours

  • Monday-Friday
  • 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Why Choose Our School

  • Safe and peaceful learning environment.
  • Experienced and caring teachers.
  • Strong academic foundation.
  • Focus on discipliine and character building.
  • Affordable and community-centered.
